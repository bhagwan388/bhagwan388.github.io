🔍 What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that can think, reason, and make decisions. From smart assistants to self-driving cars, AI is changing the world.

🧠 Key Concepts

  • Machine Learning – Learning from data without being explicitly programmed.
  • Deep Learning – Neural networks that mimic the human brain.
  • Computer Vision – Teaching machines to "see" and interpret images.
